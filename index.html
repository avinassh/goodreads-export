What we do

We are an amazing, revolutionary data science startup. We deal with tera bytes of data every hour. Our main service is, we help moving your Goodreads library to Libib. Libib is a revolutionary startup, just like M&S. And it will change your life and the way you think about books.

Process

  1. Provide us your email ID and link to your Goodreads account.
  2. Once it is passed through our revolutionary algorithm(patent pending), we will send bunch of files, each corresponding to your Goodreads book shelf. For example, if you have three shelves, like read, reading, naughty then you will receive 3 corresponding files i.e. reading.csv, reading.csv and naughty.csv
  3. Then go to Libib.com, create a new library for each book shelf
  4. Go to Add items, select CSV Import and upload the CSV file for each library
